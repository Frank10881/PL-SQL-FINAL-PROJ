## Overview
The Customer Loyalty Points & Rewards Automation System generates valuable business intelligence that can drive strategic decision-making. This document outlines the key performance indicators (KPIs), reporting requirements, and dashboard needs for the system.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Customer Engagement Metrics
* **Active Customer Rate**: Percentage of customers who have made purchases in the last 30 days
* **Customer Retention Rate**: Percentage of customers who continue to make purchases over time
* **Average Customer Lifetime Value**: Total revenue generated per customer over their relationship with the business
* **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Average cost to acquire a new loyalty program member

### 2. Loyalty Program Performance Metrics
* **Points Issued Rate**: Number of loyalty points issued per day/week/month
* **Points Redemption Rate**: Percentage of issued points that are actually redeemed
* **Reward Redemption Frequency**: Average number of rewards redeemed per active customer
* **Points Expiration Rate**: Percentage of points that expire without being redeemed

### 3. Revenue and Sales Metrics
* **Loyalty Program Revenue Impact**: Increase in sales attributed to the loyalty program
* **Average Purchase Value**: Average transaction amount for loyalty program members vs. non-members
* **Purchase Frequency**: How often loyalty members make purchases compared to baseline
* **Seasonal Sales Trends**: Sales patterns correlated with loyalty program activities

### 4. Reward Program Effectiveness Metrics
* **Reward Utilization Rate**: Percentage of available rewards that are being claimed
* **Top Performing Rewards**: Ranking of rewards by popularity and redemption rate
* **Cost Per Reward Claimed**: Average cost to the business for each redeemed reward
* **Reward Category Performance**: Performance breakdown by reward type (discounts, free items, experiences)
